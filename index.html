The Artist

Known as "The Girl on Fire" — a luxury fire performer who transforms any event into an unforgettable spectacle.

Flames of Passion Entertainment delivers bespoke fire performances that fuse bold athleticism with refined artistry. From intimate beachside ceremonies to high-profile galas and festival stages, every show is meticulously crafted to captivate, elevate, and leave audiences breathless.

Traveling nationwide and beyond, Daisy brings fiery elegance and unmatched energy to every performance — fire spinning, acrobatic handstands, fire staffs, poi, fans, and full stunt productions. Fully customizable to match your event's theme, aesthetic, and vision.
